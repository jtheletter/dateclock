LUNISOLAR CALENDAR CLOCK

An holistic view of time’s cyclic progres­sion.

Touch hands and digits to show names.

Touch toggles to change month, time, ori­en­ta­tion, and theme.

All dials’ initial units are aligned:

  • The first second of a minute is zero.
  • The first minute of an hour is zero.
  • The first hour of a day is zero.
  • The first day of a month is one.
  • The first month of a year is one.
